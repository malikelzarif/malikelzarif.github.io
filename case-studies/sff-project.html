Organization: PureGym US (formerly Blink Fitness)
Date: September 2025 – Present

Project Overview

Led the end-to-end rollout of Small Form Factor (SFF) devices across 60+ fitness club locations as part of the Blink Fitness to PureGym US integration. This project focused on creating a standardized, repeatable, and fully documented deployment process to ensure operational consistency across all field environments.

Scope of Work

  • Large-scale deployment of SFF devices across 60+ club locations
  • Standardized device configuration and validation
  • Exerp system registration and verification
  • Remote monitoring and support readiness
  • Deployment documentation and tracking
  • Coordination with field teams and stakeholders

Key Responsibilities & Contributions

  • Owned the full lifecycle of SFF device deployment from staging to go-live
  • Configured Windows 10/11 systems and required .NET Framework versions (3.5 & 4.8)
  • Ensured devices were properly registered and operational within Exerp
  • Implemented TeamViewer and Remote Desktop access for ongoing support
  • Managed device serial numbers, inventory, and deployment logs
  • Validated network connectivity, Ethernet setup, and endpoint readiness
  • Developed and maintained SOPs for consistent multi-site deployments
  • Worked cross-functionally with operations, vendors, and IT leadership

Operational Impact & Outcome

Successfully delivered a consistent and scalable SFF deployment model that ensured every club launched with fully operational systems. The standardized approach reduced deployment errors, improved troubleshooting efficiency, and enabled rapid expansion while maintaining operational reliability across all locations.

Technologies & Skills

  • Windows 10 / Windows 11
  • .NET Framework 3.5 & 4.8
  • TeamViewer & Remote Desktop
  • MDM / Device Account Management
  • IT Asset Deployment & Configuration
  • Network & Ethernet Setup
  • SOP Creation & Process Standardization
  • Multi-Site IT Deployment Coordination
  • Deployment Documentation & QA Validation
