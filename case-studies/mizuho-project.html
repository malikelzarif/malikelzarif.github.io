Organization: Mizuho Bank
Associated With: Pro4ia
Date: March 2019 – March 2020

Project Overview

Led the end-to-end deployment of enterprise IT infrastructure for Mizuho Bank’s newly established New York City office. The engagement focused on delivering a secure, compliant, and fully operational technology environment from the ground up to support executive leadership, operational teams, and mission-critical financial systems.

The project demanded strict adherence to financial-industry security standards, precise coordination across multiple technical domains, and hands-on leadership during installation, testing, and go-live operations.

Scope of Work

  • Greenfield IT infrastructure deployment for a new NYC office
  • Enterprise desktop, laptop, and peripheral provisioning
  • Network equipment installation and connectivity validation
  • VoIP phone system deployment and configuration
  • Conference room AV and collaboration system setup
  • Security, compliance, and operational readiness validation

Key Responsibilities & Contributions

  • Deployed and configured desktops, laptops, printers, and networking equipment
  • Installed, tested, and supported VoIP phone systems and conference room AV setups
  • Ensured alignment with Mizuho’s financial security and compliance standards
  • Provided white-glove executive and end-user support during office go-live
  • Led and coordinated junior technicians throughout installation and rollout
  • Performed post-deployment testing, validation, documentation, and remediation

Impact & Outcome

Successfully delivered a fully operational NYC office environment on schedule, enabling seamless business operations from day one. The deployment met strict financial-industry security requirements while providing a stable, high-performance user experience across all departments.
